### What We Do

The VR market is evolving fast — new headsets every few months, hundreds of games, and prices that vary wildly across retailers. VRRare helps you cut through the noise with honest, detailed reviews and real price tracking.

No paid placements. No "every headset is amazing" reviews. Just straightforward analysis of what's worth your money.

### Reviews & Comparisons

🥽 **Headset Reviews** — In-depth analysis of every major VR headset: Meta Quest 3, Apple Vision Pro, PlayStation VR2, Valve Index, Pimax Crystal, Bigscreen Beyond, and more.

⚔️ **Head-to-Head Comparisons** — Quest 3 vs Quest 3S? PSVR2 vs Quest 3? We break down the differences that actually matter.

🎮 **Game Reviews** — 200+ VR game reviews with ratings, compatibility info, and honest opinions.
